About Us

25 Years of Experience,
Right Behind Every Job

Superior Tree and Stump is owner-operated by Dion, a qualified arborist with 25 years of hands-on experience across Adelaide and its surrounding suburbs. While the business is new, the expertise behind it isn't — Dion has spent a quarter of a century working with trees of every size, in every situation, across South Australia.

What sets us apart is our specialisation in council approval requirements under South Australian regulations. Tree work in Adelaide often requires permits that most homeowners don't know how to navigate — we handle that process, ensure full compliance, and complete every job to a standard that holds up to scrutiny.
