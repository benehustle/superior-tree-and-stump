Our Story

The People and Values Behind the Work

Superior Tree and Stump is owner-operated by Dion, a qualified arborist with 25 years of hands-on experience across Adelaide and its surrounding suburbs. While the business is new, the expertise behind it is not, that experience comes from a quarter of a century working with trees of every size, in every situation, across South Australia.

What sets us apart is straightforward service. We show up, assess the job properly, explain the safest option, and carry out the work to a high standard. If approvals or regulations need to be discussed, we cover that during the quote based on the exact property and tree involved.

Every job is personally supervised by Dion. Fully insured, straight-talking, and focused on doing the work properly rather than just quickly. From a single stump grind to a complex emergency removal after a storm, we bring the same level of care and professionalism to every property we work on.
